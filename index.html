iOS bashing be damned: Why I switched from Android to iOS in 2013

......and I'm staying even after iOS 7 happened. These are my reasons for staying on iOS, not why you should switch. If you tell me X is the best smartphone platform, I will laugh at you - because there is no clear winner, and the best depends on the requirements of the user.

The biggest reason I switched and am hesitant to go back to Android: phones are too important to fail frequently - they're mission critical. Sometimes, your life depends on it. Random battery drains, hangups, GPS failing to lock on - things like these exist even on the latest version of Android. Version 4.2 had a Bluetooth issue, and so far, it looks like 4.3 has wifi problems on some devices.
